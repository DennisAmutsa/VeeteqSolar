About Veeteq Solar

Leading the solar revolution with innovative clean energy solutions since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Veeteq Solar began as a small family business with a big vision: to make clean, renewable energy accessible to everyone. What started as a two-person operation has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted solar installation companies.

Over the past few years, we've installed more than 1200 solar systems, helping families and businesses reduce their carbon footprint while saving thousands on energy costs. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction has made us a leader in the solar industry.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our services, staying at the forefront of solar technology to provide our customers with the most efficient and cost-effective solutions available.
